About CatchLux

CatchLux is a friendly women's catchball community based in Luxembourg. Our goal is simple: bring women together to play regularly, have fun, stay active, and build friendships.

We are not a business and operate on a not-for-profit basis. The participation fee only covers the cost of renting the sports hall.

Welcome Message

Hi everyone! 💙❤️

We're so excited to launch CatchLux and build a friendly women's catchball community here in Luxembourg!

Our goal is simple: to create a fun group that can meet and play together on a regular basis. We're not a business and not-for-profit – we're simply covering the cost of renting the sports hall.

Our first trial training session will take place on Thursday, 23 July, and we'd love for you to join us!

To reserve your spot, please send the €12 participation fee via Payconiq. The fee only covers the hall rental. Once payment is received, your place is confirmed.

There are only 14 places, so registration is first come, first served.

If you have any questions or would like to know more about catchball, feel free to message us anytime.

We can't wait to meet you all! 🏐
